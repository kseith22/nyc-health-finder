NYC Nurse-Family Partnership gives first-time parents (up to 28 weeks pregnant, who qualify for WIC or Medicaid) a specially trained nurse for guidance and support through pregnancy and the child's first two years. Call 347-396-7979 or email nycnfp@health.nyc.gov to check eligibility.
