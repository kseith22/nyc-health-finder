WIC provides nutritious foods, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support to low-income pregnant, postpartum, and breastfeeding parents and children up to age five. Families on SNAP, Medicaid, or other benefits may automatically qualify; call the Growing Up Healthy Hotline at 1-800-522-5006 to find a local office.
