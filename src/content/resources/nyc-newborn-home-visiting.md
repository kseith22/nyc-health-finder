The Newborn Home Visiting Program provides no-cost visits from trained staff to support new and expecting parents with newborn care, feeding, and links to other services, in person or virtually. It is part of the Health Department's New Family Home Visits Initiative.
Source: https://www.nyc.gov/site/doh/health/health-topics/pregnancy-newborn-visiting.page
