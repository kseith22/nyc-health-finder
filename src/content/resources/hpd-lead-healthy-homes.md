The Primary Prevention Program reduces lead-based paint hazards in eligible residential buildings built before 1960 to protect children from lead poisoning. Call 311 and ask for Healthy Homes information, or email the program directly. Source: https://www.nyc.gov/site/hpd/services-and-information/lead-hazard-reduction-and-healthy-homes-program.page
